Ideas for Every Day for 30 Days

Things to do

  • Read the Bible for 30 minutes (start with Psalms)
  • Track everything you eat in a nutrition journal
  • Pray for 30 minutes
  • Do 100 pushups
  • Journal for 30 minutes
  • Code for 30 minutes
  • Run for 15 minutes
  • Write a blog post of any length
  • Read a non-Bible book for 30 minutes
  • Track all of our expenses using Mint

How to do those things

  • Stagger things, don't start them all at the same time
  • Track the things and the days and whether or not I did them using this Tracking Spreadsheet
  • Write at least a sentence or phrase about how it's going, every day

30 days. I've decided to go with Every Day for 30 Days. As for the actual things to do every day for 30 days, I think I'll start with:

  1. Read the Bible for 30 minutes
  2. Do 100 pushups

It might be kind of a lot to start with 2 at once. I guess we'll see. I'm also going to be tracking whether or not I complete the activity on a daily cadence using this Google Sheet. I will track the date, activity, day #, whether I completed it, and a quick note about how the day was. I'm sure this will evolve and change over time, but those are the things I think I will want to track starting out. I just hope I keep up with the activities and the tracker.
